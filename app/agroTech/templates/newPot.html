    Getting started is quick and easy.

    Begin by first making sure your AgroTech SmartBench is connected and your pot is mounted on top. Fill the grow pot with the correct soil for the plant species. Make sure sensors are in place before adding the plant to your collection. Allow for system to start up, then proceed to installation process if it is your first use, otherwise continue on with the AgroTech software. To build your own SmartBench check out the Information page and download the assembly instructions.



    Setup

    Follow all setup and maintance indications provided with the Agro SmartBench or found on the AgroTech repository.


    Grow

    Choose from hundreds of different plant species or use custom settings. Specific care instructions are available for almost every plant.


    Reuse

    AgroTech works to help the enviornment by providing reusable and adaptable products. Remember to always recycle.


    Share

    Show family and friends your personal grows! Use the hastag #agrotech to be included on our social media pages as well!



    Please keep in mind that a plant's health depends and varies on many enviornmental factors. Not all plants may respond the same to certain products or growing tecniques. AgroTech aims to provide accurate information for every type of plant. Note that some care indications are approximations and may require modified care per case. Love and attention are always still necessary to obtain thriving results.

    Click below on one of the buttons to choose your plant type and begin growing!
